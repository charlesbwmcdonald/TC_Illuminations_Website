Product Description

Our 18ft Premium Christmas Tree is the perfect choice for residential homes, businesses, and outdoor displays looking to make a stunning holiday statement. Handcrafted in Ocala, Florida, this magnificent tree features over 1000 premium commercial-grade LED lights that illuminate your space with holiday magic.

This tree size strikes the perfect balance between impressive visual impact and practical installation. It's ideal for:

  • Large residential front yards and estates
  • Business storefronts and office buildings
  • Community centers and public spaces
  • Churches and places of worship
  • Shopping centers and retail locations

Every rental includes complete professional installation, all necessary equipment and materials, maintenance throughout the season, and full breakdown and removal when the holidays are over. You simply enjoy the magic while we handle all the work.
